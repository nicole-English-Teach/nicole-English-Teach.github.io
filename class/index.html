Welcome

Welcome to Nicole's English Classes! I’m excited to help you improve your English skills in a friendly and personalized setting. Whether you're aiming to boost your fluency, enhance your pronunciation, or start from scratch, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. Class Options

Grammar Class: Master the building blocks of English 

Engaging, interactive lessons that cover everything from basic to advanced topics, helping learners of all levels improve their English skills and confidently apply them in real-life situations.

Learn more
Conversation Class: Speak English with Confidence

Dynamic, interactive sessions where you can practice speaking English fluently and confidently on a variety of topics, with personalized feedback to help you improve vocabulary, pronunciation, and overall communication skills in real-life situations.

Learn more
Pronunciation Class: Speak clearly and be understood

Classes are led by a trained speech-language pathologist and provide targeted exercises and personalized coaching to help you improve your accent, use stress and intonation effectively, and achieve clear, confident English speech that is easily understood by native speakers.

Learn more
Classes for Children: Learning with fun and confidence

Classes for children ages 4 to 16 provide engaging, age-appropriate lessons that build language skills through fun activities for young learners, expand vocabulary and communication for older children, and focus on real-world language use and critical thinking for teens, all in a supportive and nurturing environment.
