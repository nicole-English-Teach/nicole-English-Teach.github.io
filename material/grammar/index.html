Grammar Made Easy

Helps you understand English tenses step by step. Each section explains how and when to use a tense, with simple examples and practice activities. Choose a topic below and start mastering past, present, future and conditional forms.

The Past Tense

Learn how to talk about actions or events that happened and are finished. Understand how to tell stories, share experiences, and describe what once was. 

Learn Past Tense

The Conditional Tense

Explore how to talk about possibilities, dreams, and “what if” situations. Practice using would, could, and if-clauses in natural contexts.

Learn Conditional Tense

The Present Tense

Understand how to describe what happens now, habits, and general facts. Express daily habits, routines, and facts with clarity and confidence.

Learn Present Tense

The Future Tense

Learn how to talk about plans, predictions, and promises. Explore forms like will and going to with practical usage tips.

Learn Future Tense
